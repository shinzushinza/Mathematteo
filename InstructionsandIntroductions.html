Test your math knowledge with "MatheMatteo"

A multiple choice game that features the section’s star student

as you answer questions with him for his experiment and puts the “fun” in quadratic FUNctions.

Beginning your adventure is easy.

First, choose a character to play as.

Once you’ve done that, all you need to do is press start and the first question should pop up on your screen.

The choices can be found just below the question, and the one you select or click will be your final answer.

Get more than half of the questions right and you win!
